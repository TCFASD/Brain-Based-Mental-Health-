The Community of Practice (CoP) attached to the Brain-Based Mental Health site is specifically for mental health clinicians. Caregivers and self-advocates are not the membership audience.

**Why:** The CoP exists for clinician capacity-building and peer learning. Broadening the audience would dilute its purpose.

**How to apply:** Continued learning as a value belongs at the Home level for everyone, since the entire field is evolving and visitors deserve to know that. The CoP is the clinician-specific expression of that value, not the only carrier of it. So on Home, the continued-learning framing should stand on its own as a stance (no CoP CTA wedged in). The CoP gets a brief mention in About Us > Partnerships and full treatment on the Clinicians page, not on Home.
