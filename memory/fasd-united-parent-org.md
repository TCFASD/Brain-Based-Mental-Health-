The Brain-Based Mental Health website represents the FASD United Mental Health Work Group, which sits inside FASD United as the parent organization.

**Why:** FASD United gives the work its institutional home and authority. Making the parent-org connection visible builds trust and signals legitimacy to clinicians, caregivers, and self-advocates landing on the site.

**How to apply:** Use FASD United.png (in the pictures folder) as a recurring visual anchor: site header or footer, About Us page, and any partnership references. Treat it as a theme element, not a one-off logo placement. When designing pages, integrate the FASD United visual identity rather than presenting this as a standalone resource.

Other affiliate org logos available in the pictures folder: FASCETS.png, FASDFocus.png, Papillion Center.png, The Florida Center for Early Childhood.png, wonderlands.png. (Flow Counseling and Formed Families Forward logos may not be in the folder yet — verify before using.)
