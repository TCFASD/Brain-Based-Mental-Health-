## III. Discussion: the Clinicians page (40 min)

Exploratory questions. The goal is to think together, not necessarily to land on every decision today.

### 1. The pathway as a whole — does it work, what is missing, does it need to change? (25 min)
- Does the flow from Step 1 to Step 5 make sense? Anything that feels off, redundant, or out of order?
- Are parts of the pathway missing? What would you add, and where? Are there clinician needs we are not yet addressing?
- We are thinking about future directions (research, educational navigation for mental health clinicians, and more). Do these fit inside the existing five steps, or do they suggest the pathway should change shape — different steps, new sections, a different organizing structure?
- How do we address diagnosis when we are thinking brain first? Does it deserve its own step, or do we address it inside Step 2 (Assessments through a Brain-Based Lens)? How much weight should the page give to working without a formal diagnosis vs. with one?

### 2. The four pillars: Learn, Reflect, Apply, Check In (5 min)
- Does this structure work for the kinds of content we have (handouts, hoped-for videos, reflective prompts, self-assessment items)?
- Where does it feel forced?
- Would you add, rename, or merge any pillars?

### 3. What 10-minute video topics should we include? (10 min)
- Each pathway step has suggested video topics on the mockup. What would you add or change?
- Which topics feel most urgent to make first?
- Who is best positioned to develop each one?
